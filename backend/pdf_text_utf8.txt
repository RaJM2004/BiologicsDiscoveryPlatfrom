1. Executive Summary  
The Biologics Discovery Platform  is an AI -powered drug discovery system that 
automates the end -to-end workflow from target identification to wet -lab validation. The 
current MVP prototype  demonstrates the complete scientific pipeline across five modules:  
Module  Function  MVP Status  
Target Explorer  Identify & visualize protein 
targets (3D)     Functional (RCSB PDB)  
Hit Screening  Screen compound libraries 
for drug candidates     Functional (XGBoost + 
RDKit)  
Lead Optimization  Evolve molecules using AI 
for better drug  properties     Functional (Genetic 
Algorithm)  
Wet -Lab Validation  Orchestrate physical 
experiments with lab robots      Simulated (Webhook -
ready)  
Blinded Results  Unbiased analysis of 
experimental data     Functional (Hash -based 
blinding)  
Will This Platform Help Scientists in Drug Discovery?  
Yes — the platform’s architecture and workflow are scientifically valid.  The pipeline 
follows the industry -standard drug discovery funnel:  
1,000,000 compounds → Scree ning → 100 Hits → Optimization → 5 Leads → Wet -Lab 
→ 1 Drug Candidate  
[!IMPORTANT] However, the current MVP uses simplified AI models and 
synthetic data . To be genuinely useful for scientists and produce results trusted 
for real drug development, the platf orm requires upgrades across data quality, 
AI model depth, regulatory compliance, and infrastructure scale  — all 
detailed in this document.

“clinical -ready”
